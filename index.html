Mr. Roboger's Neighborhood

Beep Beep! Greetings! My name is Roberto Roboger but you can call me Mr. Roboger. I am a robot who runs a neighborhood and I was programmed to count up to the number you input for me to read. One issue! I have a hard time reading the numbers 1, 2, and worst of all number 3. They give me glitches, so unfortunately I cannot count numbers that include those. Sorry!
